About Our School

Learn more about our mission, vision, and the values that guide us.

Welcome to Mount Gerizim

Residential developments in Kasoa and its environs are sprawling with the population growing at a very fast rate. However, the government has not been able to keep up with the population growth rate in terms of the provision of school infrastructure for basic schools for children of school going ages. This has resulted in overcrowding in classrooms with its attendant problems of poor performance in basic literacy and mathematics. This has resulted in the mushrooming of private schools in the area with inadequate to non-existent infrastructure to cater for the needs of students.

Mount Gerizim is a newly built school in Kasoa, closer to the Obom - Domeabra area to serve the needs of parents who want quality yet affordable schools with adequate basic infrastructure for their wards. This includes spacious, well ventilated, well-illuminated classrooms furnished with modern classroom furniture to facilitate teaching and learning.

Curriculum

The school is currently pursuing the Ghana Education Service curriculum from Kindergarten to Basic 9.

  • Early Childhood - from 18 months
  • Primary – Basic 1 to Basic 6
  • Junior High – Basic 7 to Basic 9

Vision & Mission

Our Vision is to provide a welcoming, happy and safe environment for the development of well rounded, confident and responsible individuals who aspire to achieve their full potential.

Our Key Objectives are

  • To provide the tools and materials essential for teaching and learning as well as the overall development of learners.
  • To establish clear targets and set high expectations, academic and non-academic, for our students and teachers.
  • To ensure teachers are adequately trained to handle their work.
  • To deliver high quality teaching and learning programs aligned to the GES Curriculum and Assessment Outline.
  • To establish a learning environment that is welcoming, safe, caring and inclusive.

Our Values & Beliefs

Our motto - INTELLECT WITH CHARACTER. This is underpinned by the following values:

Respect – We treat others with consideration and regard.

Honesty – We show integrity by being honest, trustworthy and fair.

Hard Work – We try hard and strive to achieve excellence.

Our Beliefs About Learning

We believe that...

  • All students can be confident and successful learners.
  • Students learn best when motivated, engaged and challenged.
  • Everyone is a learner and it is a lifelong process.
  • Students need to take responsibility for their own learning by being reflective and setting goals for improvement.

Our Teaching Staff

Our teaching staff are qualified, well trained, experienced and motivated to nurture our learners to achieve their full potential and inspire confidence in them.
