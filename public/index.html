About Our School

Mount Gerizim is a newly built school in Kasoa, closer to the Obom - Domeabra area, serving parents who want quality yet affordable schools. We offer spacious, well-ventilated, and well-illuminated classrooms with modern furniture to facilitate effective teaching and learning.

Our vision is to provide a welcoming, happy, and safe environment for developing well-rounded, confident, and responsible individuals to achieve their full potential.
